Country Licenses For Nursing Over Time





The data highlights trends in nursing staff across Denmark, Italy, Korea, New Zealand, and the United States from 2018 to 2022. Denmark shows steady growth in nurse counts until 2020, after which data is missing for later years. Italy maintains consistently high numbers around 450,000, with slight annual increases. The United States stands out with the highest numbers, climbing steadily from nearly 4.7 million in 2018 to over 5.3 million in 2022. In a racing bar chart, these trends would show the U.S. consistently leading, followed by Italy and Korea, with fluctuations in Denmark and New Zealand due to missing data.
